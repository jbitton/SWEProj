Welcome to your dashboard!


This is your schedule planner. In the taskbar, you have the option of letting the application generate a schedule for you, or to view your drafts you have saved. You have a maximum of 5 drafts allowed to be saved in your profile. Team 1023MB hopes you enjoy the experience!

For the application to work, our team developed an algorithm that will pick your schedule for you based on what classes you need to take. Once a sample schedule is generated, drag and drop can be utilized to move other courses in the schedule so that you may save it as a draft, if you choose to not go directly with the schedule we have provided.
